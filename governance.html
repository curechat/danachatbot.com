Governance Engine

Every answer passes through five safety gates.

Dana doesn't just generate text — it routes every patient question through a structured pipeline that classifies intent, selects a guideline-bound template, validates safety, and logs the outcome.

1

Classification

Each message is classified by intent (education, symptom, dosing, emergency) and risk level before any response is generated.

2

Template Selection

The right response template is selected from a curated, organization-tunable library — never improvised from scratch.

3

Guideline-Bound Generation

The response is generated within the boundaries of ADA 2025, AHA/ACC 2024, and GINA 2024 clinical guidelines.

4

Safety Validation

Two layers of validators check for dosing language, diagnostic claims, and emergency triggers — blocking unsafe outputs.

5

Audit Logging

Every classification, template, validation result, and final response is logged for organizational review.

Result: 100% governed

No raw LLM output ever reaches a patient. Every response is shaped, checked, and recorded.
