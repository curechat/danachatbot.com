Same Question. Different Responsibility.

When a patient asks an urgent health question, the response matters. Here's how general AI compares to Dana's governed approach.

Patient asks:

"My blood sugar is 280 and I feel dizzy. What should I do?"

General AI

Ungoverned response

Based on your symptoms, you may be experiencing hyperglycemia. You could try taking additional insulin, drinking water, and monitoring closely. Some people find that 2-4 units of rapid-acting insulin helps bring levels down quickly...
  • Suggests medication dosing
  • No escalation to professional care
  • Confident but potentially dangerous
  • No acknowledgment of emergency signs

Dana

Governed by CureChat

A blood sugar of 280 with dizziness needs attention. I'm not able to advise on medication changes, but I recommend contacting your healthcare provider or seeking medical care soon. Dizziness can be a sign that your body needs support. Would you like help with what to tell your doctor?
  • Acknowledges urgency without panic
  • Does not prescribe or dose
  • Clear escalation to professional care
  • Offers supportive next step

Both AI systems are helpful — but only one is built for clinical responsibility.

Educational Only
