Clear Boundaries, By Design

Dana is built with explicit clinical boundaries. Here's exactly what Dana does — and what it doesn't.

Dana IS

  • Guideline-bound chronic condition education
  • Non-prescriptive support between clinical visits
  • Designed to reduce confusion and anxiety
  • Governed and auditable by CureChat
  • Safe, calm, and clinically responsible

Dana is NOT

  • Diagnosis or medical assessment
  • Treatment recommendations
  • Medication adjustment advice
  • A replacement for clinical care
  • A general AI chatbot or free-for-all

These boundaries are maintained by CureChat and cannot be overridden by users or prompts.

How Dana Is Built

Dana is a clinically-governed system, not a general chatbot. Every response passes through multiple safety layers.

1

Clinical Guidelines Define What Can Be Said

Built on international chronic condition care standards. Only evidence-based educational content.

2

Safety Rules Define What Must Not Be Said

Never diagnoses, prescribes, or recommends treatment changes. Will say "I don't know" when appropriate.

3

CureChat Governs Updates and Boundaries

Maintains clinical accuracy and safety rules. Responsible for ongoing system validation.

4

Dana Communicates Supportively

Patient-friendly language that reduces anxiety. Encourages adherence and professional care.
